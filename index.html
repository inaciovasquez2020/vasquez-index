General Program

Abstract: I develop and maintain the Unified Rigidity Framework (URF), a formal research program exploring the structural limits of physical and computational systems. The framework posits that finite information capacity and locality act as fundamental obstructions to arbitrary refinement, leading to "rigidity" in model selection and predictive control.
