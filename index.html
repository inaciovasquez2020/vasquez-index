Unified Rigidity Framework (URF)

The Unified Rigidity Framework (URF) is a formally closed research program investigating the structural limits of finite information-bearing systems. By synthesizing finite model theory and spectral analysis, the framework identifies the exact boundaries of system stability.

The Local→Global Problem is now resolved: for all bounded-degree systems, global structural rigidity is a direct consequence of local homogeneity, provided the system exists outside a single, well-defined obstruction class.

Terminal Rigidity Wall
